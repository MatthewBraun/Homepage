Jailbroken iPhone

A jailbroken iPhone is an iPhone that has been "hacked" so that the internal security has been compromised and unsigned apps/tweaks can be installed without Apple's permission. The jailbreaking process is legal and is most likely the reason Apple's IOS firmware has always been advancing at a very competitive rate.

  1. Able to remove Verizon from the status bar.
  2. Able to resize and rename any App.
  3. Able to speed up animations for default responses.
  4. Able to explore the internal filesystem of IOS.
  5. Able to use Bluetooth to share files.
