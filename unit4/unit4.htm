Jailbreaking an iPhone

Knowing the iOS version of your iphone™

Here is a list of steps to find the version number on your iphone.

  1. Open "Settings" application
  2. Open "General" tab
  3. Open "About" tab
  4. Take note of the "Version" tab

Checking version number with the compatible jailbreakable database.

To check your version number navigate to www.canijailbreak.com 

				On this page you should see multiple iOS version numbers.
				Compare your version number to the iOS number on this page.
				If there is a ✔ then you are able to jailbreak your iPhone.

If you want to proceed with the jailbreak.

A couple of things to rememeber during the process of jailbrakeing.

  • You might have to restore your iphone
  • You should make a back up of all your data first
  • Verify the data is backed up before proceeding
  • Obtain more informtion about the jailbreaking community
